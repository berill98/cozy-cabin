About the Cozy Cabin

The Cozy Cabin is set on the edge of a forest near the beautiful small village of Barrmill, just 1 hour south of Glasgow.

Cozy Cabin is a classic “A-frame” style weekendhouse designed with sustainability and the use of natural materials in mind. In the interior design, we focused on Scandinavian simplicity and to maximize comfort and relaxation. It is full of character with original open beams, wood burning stove and big picture windows on all sides with views to the woods and mountains.

Downstairs you can find a small, fully equipped kitchen with a fireplace, a microwave and a dining area. Here is the the living room with a flat-screen TV and the private bathroom with shower and a hairdryer. A fridge, an oven and stovetop are also offered, as well as a kettle. Upstairs is a bedroom that can comfortably accommodate up to four people.

Come and relax in the woods with a book or grab your hiking boots or bikes and explore the surrounding countryside. Perfect for wonderful day trips to Highland castles and magnificent mountains. We're just a 20 minute drive from the coast which has stunning cliff top walks and beautiful little bays and fishing villages.
